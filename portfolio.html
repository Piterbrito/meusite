Briefly about Myself

Data Analyst with background in Mechanical Engineering, Business Development and Project Management. While at UC Berkeley I conducted Financial data analysis research through Python using Public data to predict profits outcome on test data set, then worked with subsets of “revenue” to determine average, normal distribution impact overtime with given metrics. Through this research, I was able to outline the odds for future purchases by Gender, Item, Demographic and Persona. This project, along with others, helped me realize I wanted to use my Creative and Management skills within a company to help develop great products.

I earned a certificate in Data Visualization from Berkeley where I worked with various types of libraries’, programming in Python and SQL, manipulating Pandas dataframes and visualizing the results in Matplotlib. I believe these skills combined with my leadership and business acumen make me an advantageous candidate and good fit for any firm.
